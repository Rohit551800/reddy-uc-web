Overview

Influenza (flu) is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. It spreads easily and can cause symptoms ranging from mild to severe, often hitting suddenly. At Reddy Urgent Care, we can test for flu on-site with rapid results and begin treatment the same day without the cost or wait of an emergency room.

Symptoms We Treat

  • Sudden fever (100°F or higher)
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Body aches and muscle pain
  • Sore throat
  • Chills and sweating
  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Headache
  • Dry cough

How We Treat It at Reddy Urgent Care

Assessment

A provider will review your symptoms and check your temperature and vital signs.

Rapid Flu Test

A quick nasal swab test (Flu A/B) gives results in minutes, right here at the clinic.

Treatment

If confirmed, antiviral medication (such as Tamiflu) may be prescribed. Supportive care recommendations will be provided for rest, hydration, and symptom relief.

Follow-up Guidance

You'll leave with clear instructions on what to watch for and when to seek further care.

Types Of Flu We Treat

  • Influenza AThe most common and often most severe type. Responsible for most seasonal flu outbreaks.
  • Influenza BGenerally milder than Type A but still causes significant illness, especially in children.
  • Flu with Secondary InfectionSome flu cases develop into secondary bacterial infections such as ear infections, sinus infections, or pneumonia.
  • Flu-Like IllnessNot all flu-like symptoms are influenza. We can test to confirm and treat accordingly.

When to Go to the Emergency Room Instead

Go to the nearest emergency room or call 911 if you have:

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Persistent chest pain or pressure
  • Confusion, disorientation, or altered mental status
  • Severe or persistent vomiting that prevents keeping fluids down
  • Signs of dehydration (no urination, dry mouth, dizziness)
  • Bluish lips or face

If you are unsure, go to the ER. It is always better to be safe.
