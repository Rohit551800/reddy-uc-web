About Us

Who We Are

At Reddy Urgent Care, we believe that quality medical care should be within reach for everyone, regardless of age, background, or insurance status. Founded with a commitment to honest, accessible urgent care, our clinics are led by a board-certified emergency medicine physician supported by qualified nurse practitioners at every location.

We are not an emergency room, and we are not a primary care practice. We are your community's urgent care, here for the moments that cannot wait until tomorrow. Whether it is a child with a high fever, an adult with a sprained ankle, or a working professional who needs a rapid test before returning to the office, we are ready when you walk in.

With walk-in clinics across Long Beach and Paramount, and patients regularly visiting from Lakewood, Carson, Wilmington, Lynwood, and the wider South Bay area, Reddy Urgent Care is proud to be a trusted healthcare resource in the Greater Los Angeles community.

Our Values

We believe every patient deserves fast, honest, and respectful care — delivered without the wait, cost, or complexity of an emergency room. We are transparent about what we can and cannot treat, clear about insurance coverage before we begin, and committed to making every visit as straightforward as possible.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted urgent care provider in the Long Beach and South Bay community — known not just for how fast we see patients, but for the quality and honesty of the care we deliver. We aim to be the first call for families, workers, and individuals who need same-day medical attention and deserve to be treated with skill and respect.

Our Team

Our providers bring skill, experience, and genuine care to every patient interaction. Walk in and you will be seen by qualified clinicians who take the time to understand your symptoms and get you the right treatment — every visit, every location.
