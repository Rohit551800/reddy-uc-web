Ear, Nose & Throat

Ear infections, sore throats, nasal congestion, and swollen glands are among the most common reasons patients visit urgent care. At Reddy Urgent Care, we diagnose and treat ENT conditions the same day — with on-site rapid strep and mono testing available. No referral, no appointment needed.

Whether it's a child with an ear infection or an adult with a persistent sore throat, we see patients of all ages.

Respiratory

Respiratory illnesses can come on fast and affect your daily life significantly. At Reddy Urgent Care, we provide same-day diagnosis and treatment for a full range of respiratory conditions, from the common cold to bronchitis and mild asthma. Walk in any time during opening hours. No appointment needed.

Our providers will assess your symptoms, run rapid tests where needed (flu, COVID-19, strep), and begin treatment the same visit.
