Insurance & Payment Options

We accept most major insurance plans and welcome patients regardless of coverage type including Medi-Cal, Medicare, and self-pay. Our front desk will verify your eligibility when you arrive.

Insurance acceptance is subject to approval. Certain services including ultrasounds and IV hydration therapy may require prior authorisation or may not be covered under all plans. Coverage for X-rays also varies by insurance provider. Services are subject to real-time eligibility verification at the front desk. Please bring your Insurance card on every visit.
We will always inform you of your coverage before treatment begins.
